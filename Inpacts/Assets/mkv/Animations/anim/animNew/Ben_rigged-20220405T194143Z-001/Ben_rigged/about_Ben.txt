Main: Rig is standard biped with 'skin'modifier. A rotatable bone for lower jaw is added 'bone_jaw' 

Eyes: Linked to 'head' biped part and have 'look at' controller. Move'look_at' spline and the eyes will follow.

Tongue: Has a few Point helpers to manipulate - tongueCONTROL_Point02, tongueCONTROL_Point03 etc.
Use 'assume skin pose' command from animation menu to reset transformations.


To optimyze hair make hairs thicker and fewer in count.
